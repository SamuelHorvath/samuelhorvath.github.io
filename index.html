Biography

I am on the job market!

I am the fifth-year student of the MS/PhD program in Statistics under the supervision of professor Peter Richtárik at Visual Computing Center KAUST. During my PhD, I interned at Amazon hosted by Cedric Archambeau, at Samsung hosted by Stefanos Laskaridis, Mario Almeida and Nicolas Lane, and Facebook hosted by Michael Rabbat. My research interest is mainly in Non-convex and Convex Optimization for Machine Learning, in particular Federated Learning. Before that, I was an undergrad student in Financial Mathematics at Comenius University. I received the Best Poster Award at Data Science Summer School 2018 at Ecole Polytechnique, and I am a recipient of the Best Paper Award at Federated Learning Workshop NeurIPS 2020.
